About Centenary Baptist Church

Our heritage, beliefs, mission, and leadership

Our Story

Founded in 1880, Centenary Baptist Church Hanamkonda has been a beacon of hope and faith in Hanamkonda for nearly 145 years. Our church was established by devoted missionaries who felt called to serve this community and spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Throughout the decades, we have remained steadfast in our commitment to biblical truth, evangelical fervor, and community service. We have weathered many storms and celebrated countless victories, always trusting in God's faithfulness and provision.

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe in the divine inspiration and authority of the Holy Scriptures as God's Word to humanity.

Salvation

We believe in salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, not by works.

Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Church

We believe in the local church as God's instrument for fellowship, worship, and service.
